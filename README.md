# Borked Contact Form

A contact form designed to be intentionally frustrating and delightfully broken.

## Features
- **Name Input**: Catch falling letters to spell your name.
- **Phone Input**: Use archery to shoot numbers for your phone number.
- **Email Input**: A single-reel slot machine to pick your email address character by character.
- **Message Input**: Gravity-enabled text area where letters fall.
- **Submit Button**: A button that runs away from your cursor.

Good luck.
