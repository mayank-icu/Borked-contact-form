# Borked Contact Form

A contact form that refuses to behave like a normal contact form.

Instead of politely asking for your details, it turns every input into a tiny, unnecessary challenge. The result is equal parts annoying, entertaining, and completely impractical.

Live demo: https://borked.netlify.app

## What It Does

Each field works in the worst possible way:

- **Name Input**  
  Letters fall from the top of the screen and you have to catch them to spell your name.

- **Phone Input**  
  Use archery mechanics to shoot the numbers of your phone number.

- **Email Input**  
  A single-reel slot machine that lets you select your email one character at a time.

- **Message Input**  
  A gravity-based text area where letters drop as you type.

- **Submit Button**  
  The button actively runs away from your cursor when you try to click it.

## Running It Locally

No build tools. No setup headaches.

1. Download or clone the repository.
2. Open the `index.html` file in your browser.

That's it.

Because most forms are boring.
